Optogram presents —
A Piet Mondrian Exhibition

From
Chaos
to
Order

To celebrate Piet Mondrian's 150th birthday, Optogram has created an immersive experience ranging from the chaotic worlds of the past through a transition to the Neoplastic vision of harmony.
Opening Spring 2022.

  • Group 08
  • Digital Art
  • From chaos to order

This is a university project by students of the Digital and Interaction Design Master's Degree to celebrate Mondrian's 150th birthday. It has no affiliation to the Mondrian foundation and was not authorised or commissioned by it or its affiliates.
